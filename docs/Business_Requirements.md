# FinSight BI - Business Requirements Document

## Project Overview

FinSight BI is an enterprise Business Intelligence platform developed to provide finance and business leaders with real-time visibility into organizational performance. The platform centralizes sales, customer, product, budget, and expense data to enable faster, data-driven decision making.

---

## Business Problem

The organization currently relies on manual Excel reports prepared by multiple departments. This process results in:

- Delayed reporting
- Inconsistent business metrics
- Limited visibility into profitability
- Difficulty tracking budget utilization
- Lack of executive-level dashboards

The absence of a centralized reporting system prevents stakeholders from making timely strategic decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of FinSight BI are:

- Centralize financial and operational data
- Provide real-time business insights
- Improve executive decision-making
- Monitor business KPIs
- Track profitability across products and regions
- Compare Budget vs Actual performance
- Reduce manual reporting effort

---

## Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|-------------|---------------|
| CEO | Monitor overall business performance |
| CFO | Financial reporting and profitability |
| Sales Director | Revenue and regional sales analysis |
| Finance Team | Budget and expense monitoring |
| Operations Manager | Operational performance tracking |

---

## Business KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Expenses
- Net Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Budget vs Actual
- Revenue Growth %
- Customer Count
- Average Order Value
- Top Performing Products
- Top Performing Regions

---

## Functional Requirements

The system must:

- Store transactional sales data
- Maintain customer information
- Track product performance
- Monitor departmental expenses
- Compare budget against actual spending
- Generate executive dashboards
- Support filtering by date, region, customer, and product

---

## Expected Business Outcomes

- Faster reporting
- Improved financial visibility
- Better budget control
- Increased profitability analysis
- Data-driven decision making
